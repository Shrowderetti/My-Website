ABOUT ME

Throughout my education, I have picked up many hobbies and explored various ideas for my career path. Here, I try to collect all my thoughts and ideas. Therefore, on this site, you will find my drawings, photos, 3D renders, and programming projects.

I've always had an artistic side, which explains my interest in photography and drawing. With these passions in mind, I initially decided to enroll at the University of Warsaw to study Architecture. However, I soon realized that the major wasn't the right fit for me, so I decided to leave the program and search for something more fulfilling.

Leaving Architecture allowed me to broaden my perspective and explore fields that had always intrigued me. That’s how I got into 3D design and a bit of programming. With these new interests, I decided to pursue another degree—this time focused on Computer Science, Graphic Design, and Web Development.

Now, as I continue my studies, my main interest is definitely 3D art. A true dream come true would be the opportunity to work in Game Development, preferably as a 3D artist. However, any experience in that kind of working environment would be ideal.

MY WORK

Over the years, through both education and personal work, I have created a wide range of artworks that I’m rather proud of. Each piece reflects a part of my growth, curiosity, and passion for the craft, and I’m happy to show it off.
