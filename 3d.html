3D renders

Ever since I discovered my first 3D software as a kid, I fell in love with it. Back then, it was SketchUp, and I used it to create all sorts of models that captured my imagination. Many years later, I discovered Blender—and that love reignited with full force. Blender became a powerful tool for me to express myself and create visually appealing art.

Here, I share only my work created in Blender. Although learning this software has been incredibly inspiring, I’m now also exploring Maya and starting to understand the basics of Houdini.
